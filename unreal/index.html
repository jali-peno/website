with the recent decision by epic to essentially turn the unreal franchise into abandonware, ive taken it upon myself to obtain physical copies of and host downloads for the classic unreal games. currently this subdomain only hosts copies of the games, but i will periodically update it to include additional goodies for fans :)


the game isos on the side are clean rips of the discs from my physical copies without any drm removed! games like unreal gold should install and run just fine by mounting the iso, meanwhile i believe ut04 and unreal ii require nocd patches.

games:

Unreal Gold (1998)

Unreal Tournament (1999)

Unreal II (2003)

Unreal Tournament (2004)

extras:

Unreal II "Play Disc"

Unreal Gold Soundtrack
